Vision of Islamic Education Management Study Program

“To become an excellent, competitive, religious and professional study program in the field of Islamic education management in ASEAN in 2023”

Mission of Islamic Education Management Study Program

  1. 1. Organizing education, teaching, and training in Islamic education management with religious and mixed approach
  2. 2. Building a research culture, innovation, and scientific publication with national and international reputation in the field of Islamic education management.
  3. 3. Conducting a community service towards the society by improving the performance of the management of Islamic educational institution both in Indonesia and abroad.
